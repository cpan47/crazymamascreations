## ‚úÖ How to Add a NEW Product

### Step 1: Create New Record
1. Scroll to the bottom of the table
2. Click the **"+"** button (or click in an empty row)
3. A form will appear with all the fields

### Step 2: Fill in Product Information

**Title** ‚≠ê REQUIRED
- Example: "Led Zeppelin - Stairway to Heaven"
- This is what customers will see as the product name

**Description** ‚≠ê REQUIRED
- Example: "Classic Led Zeppelin design on vintage blue flannel with distressed graphics"
- Describe the shirt, colors, special features

**Image** ‚≠ê REQUIRED
- Click in the Image field
- Click "Choose file" or drag-and-drop your photo
- Airtable will automatically upload and host the image!
- You can upload multiple photos - the first one will be the main image

**Category** ‚≠ê REQUIRED
- Click the dropdown
- Choose one:
  - `classic-rock` - Most band shirts (Led Zeppelin, Pink Floyd, etc.)
  - `alternative` - Alternative rock (Nirvana, Pearl Jam, etc.)
  - `punk` - Punk/Metal bands
  - `lifestyle` - Photos of people wearing shirts
  - `country` - Country music artists

**Price**
- Default is 85 (meaning $85)
- Change only if you want a different price for this item

**InStock** (Checkbox)
- ‚úÖ CHECK if the shirt is ready to ship immediately
- ‚¨ú UNCHECK if it needs to be made to order
- This shows "In Stock" or "Made to Order" on the website

**Featured** (Checkbox)
- ‚úÖ CHECK if you want this on the homepage
- ‚¨ú UNCHECK for regular products
- Only feature your best 5-7 products!

**ImagePath** (Optional - ignore this)
- Leave empty - this is only for backward compatibility

### Step 3: Save
- Click outside the record or press Enter
- Your product is now saved!
- **Website updates within 30 seconds!**

### Tip 4: Always Add Good Descriptions

## ‚ùì Troubleshooting

### "I added a product but don't see it on the website"
- Wait 30-60 seconds and refresh the website
- Make sure you filled in Title, Description, Image, and Category
- Check that you're looking at the live website (crazymamascreations.love)

### "Images aren't showing up"
- Make sure you uploaded to the "Image" field (not ImagePath)
- Check that the image file isn't corrupted
- Try re-uploading the photo

### "I can't find a product"
- Use the search bar at the top
- Check if you have any filters active (clear them)
- Sort by "Title" to see alphabetically

### "I accidentally deleted something!"
- Click the dropdown menu (top left)
- Choose "Trash"
- Find your deleted record
- Click "Restore"
- **Note: Trash empties after 7 days!**
